
After the first round, environmental hazards may occur in any country. These hazards will kill a random percentage of the army stationed at the country the hazard occurred in, up to a maximum value.
These hazards appear as icons over the countries they have affected.

Volcanic Eruption - up to 20% casualties, 10% chance
Tornado - up to 30% casualties, 12% chance
Hurricane - up to 10% casualties, 20% chance
Tsunami - up to 40% casualties, 17% chance
